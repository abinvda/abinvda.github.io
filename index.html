Projects

Reinforcement learning on planar biped robots

As my Bachelor's Thesis project, I developed a 4-link planar Biped robot. Reinforcement learning algorithm was developed to make the robot learn to walk in forward direction. Implemented Actor-Critic approach in continuous state space (learnt using fitted value iteration).

Energy-efficient compliant actuator

Under the guidance of Dr. David Braun, I developed a real-time controller for a complaint actuation mechanism. The device was a variable-stiffness joint, built for application in future biomedical research (prosthetics and exo-skeletons). I also designed PCBs for implementing the controller developed. The controller was implemented in C++ and multi-threading was used for real-time operation of the system.

Extreme Learning Neuro-Fuzzy Inference System

The research work was aimed at testing the concept of Extreme Learning Machine for training an ANFIS. This employs a fast method for training of Single-Layer Feed-forward Neural Network by generating random weights(projections) of input data and finding output using least square estimate from those projections. Work has been carried on its implementation for the classification purposes and the results have confirmed that the method is far much better than the traditional ANFIS method.

ABU-Robocon India

Leading the institute robotics team, I represented my institute at various national level robotics competitions. My work primarily included mechanism designing, robot navigation and implementing learning algorithms. In addition, I also worked on micro-controllers, sensor fusion, PCBs and various other aspects of robotics.
